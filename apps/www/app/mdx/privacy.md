# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026

Welcome to **willy.im**! Your privacy matters, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

### Information We Collect

**Analytics Data:** We collect anonymized, non-personally-identifiable analytics data to understand how visitors interact with our site.

**Google Account Data:** If you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect limited information from your Google account, including your name, email address, and profile picture. This data is used solely to authenticate you and provide a personalized experience.

**YouTube Data:** If you connect your YouTube account, we access your YouTube liked videos through the YouTube API Services. This data is used to display your liked videos on the site. Our use of YouTube API Services is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Cookies and Sessions:** We use cookies to manage authentication sessions. These cookies are strictly functional and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

### How We Use Your Data

- Analytics data is used to improve site functionality.
- Google account data is used for authentication and personalization.
- YouTube data is used to display your liked videos.

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary and do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described here.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

### Data Retention

- Anonymized analytics data is retained indefinitely.
- Account data is retained as long as your account is active.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data by contacting us.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at **admin@willy.im**.
- **Revoke** Google access by visiting your [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

### Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

### Contact

If you have questions, feel free to contact us at **admin@willy.im**.
